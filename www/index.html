Laptops and iPads

Student Computing Services provides students free access to PC laptops, MacBooks and iPads. They are located on the 4th floor. You can also check out iPads and laptops on the Mezzanine.

  • Find the check out station on the Mezzanine.
  • Find Student Computing Services on the 4th Floor.
  • More about Student Computing Services on the SJSU Library website.

Library Cards

A library card and PIN are needed to borrow items or get access to ebooks and databases. Bring your Tower Card to the Circulation Desk to get a library card.

  • Get help at the Circulation Desk on the 1st Floor.
  • More information on the Library Website.
